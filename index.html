A Few Words
About Me

My adorable career started when I was 13 and Pasquettaz, who is a friend of Paul Bocuse at Collonges au Mont d’or, notably referred me to be employed. Even though that was my first experience working in the kitchen I did very well. I learnt that the right ingredients are main factor for a delicious dish. That is why at Collonges au Mont d’or which was a high-end restaurant buying all ingredients at the market freshly cut straight from the farmers.

After that I did 2 years apprenticeship at Jean-Pierre Driss's restaurant called “La Ferme”. I was greatly happy when I found out that he makes everything onsite from bread to ice cream. Moreover, his restaurant has it's own garden in the back and he had a gardener, who collected fresh vegetables and fruits to be cooked daily. To be honest, cooking by using freshly-cut ingredients is a most astonishing thing I had ever learned.

When my apprenticeship came to the end, I went to Centre Européen de Formation and graduated with my Certificat d'Aptitude Professionnelle en Cuisine Traditionnelle, thanks to which I had got the full understanding of French cuisine. As you may know, France has many regions and each has it's own main traditional dish. The Centre Européen de Formation taught me all of them and I achieved an excellence level of knowledge ready to utilize in my services for you!
